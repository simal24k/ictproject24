Our Projects

Have a look at our recent projects:

Modern Office Building

This project focuses on creating a modern office space that fosters productivity and collaboration. The building’s design emphasizes transparency with large glass windows, providing ample natural light throughout the workspace. The layout features open-plan offices, breakout areas, and flexible meeting rooms, enhancing flexibility and adaptability for evolving work styles. The exterior design incorporates green spaces, a rooftop garden, and sustainable materials

Museum of Contemporary Art

Designed as a cultural landmark, this museum’s architecture pushes the boundaries of conventional design. The building’s striking modern facade incorporates abstract forms and irregular geometry, with expansive exhibition spaces, interactive galleries, and a public art plaza. The design takes into account the flow of natural light, enhancing the display of artwork while minimizing direct sunlight that could harm the pieces. The interior is designed to be flexible for different types of exhibitions.

Luxury Residential Tower

his project focuses on designing a luxury residential tower in the heart of a city. The design incorporates sleek, modern lines and high-end finishes, with spacious apartments offering breathtaking views. The building includes premium amenities such as a rooftop pool, fitness center, and a spa, providing a high standard of living for residents. Sustainable design features such as energy-efficient HVAC systems, solar panels, and rainwater harvesting systems ensure that the project is environmentally responsible.

Waterfront Commercial Complex

A waterfront commercial complex designed to revitalize a city’s waterfront area. The development includes retail spaces, cafes, and restaurants along with public plazas and open spaces for people to gather and relax by the water. The design focuses on creating a vibrant, pedestrian-friendly environment with easy access to the water, and the integration of sustainable, energy-efficient materials and techniques, including tidal energy for power.

Eco-Friendly Hotel Resort

A boutique eco-friendly hotel resort built in harmony with nature. The design features locally sourced materials, energy-efficient systems, and a green roof that blends seamlessly into the landscape. The hotel includes an outdoor pool, spa, and restaurant, all designed with sustainability in mind. Solar panels, rainwater harvesting systems, and natural ventilation contribute to the resort’s low environmental impact, creating a unique getaway for eco-conscious travelers.

Skybridge Residential Complex

A cutting-edge residential complex featuring interconnected towers through suspended skybridges. The design prioritizes vertical living, allowing for open communal spaces between buildings and incredible city views. This mixed-use development includes residential units, office spaces, and retail areas. The design incorporates energy-efficient solutions such as passive solar shading, green roofs, and integrated solar panels, making it a beacon of sustainable urban living.
